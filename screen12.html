2 / 4 — The Strategy
Invest the same
amount every month
$200/month, every month. You buy more shares when prices are low, fewer when high. This is DCA.
12 months of investing $200/month:
Total invested
$2,400
Avg cost/share
$46.28
Current price
$52
You paid an average of $46.28 per share even though the price ranged from $38 to $55. That's the magic — volatility works FOR you.
