2 / 4 — Your Tax Shields
Three accounts
the IRS wants you
to ignore
Each works differently. All save you thousands.
401(k) / 403(b) Pre-tax
Invest BEFORE taxes are taken. Pay taxes later when you withdraw in retirement (likely at a lower rate).
2026 limit: $23,500/year + employer match (free money)
Roth IRA Tax-free growth
Invest AFTER taxes. All growth and withdrawals are 100% tax-free forever. Best for young earners.
2026 limit: $7,000/year (income limits apply)
HSA Triple tax-free
Tax-free in, tax-free growth, tax-free out for medical. The most powerful account — if you have a high-deductible plan.
2026 limit: $4,300 individual / $8,550 family
