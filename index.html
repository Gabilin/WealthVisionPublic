Questions

Good to know.

Is my financial data really private?add
Yes. WealthVision has no backend and no accounts. Everything you enter stays in local storage on your device. Nothing is uploaded, and there are no third-party trackers or analytics.
Does it work offline?add
Completely. Every simulator, lesson, and tool runs on device. Currency formatting is read from your device locale with no network calls.
Is WealthVision financial advice?add
No. It is an educational and planning tool. All figures are illustrative and based on historical averages, which never guarantee future results.
Do you link my bank account?add
Never. WealthVision does not connect to banks, hold money, or take a cut of assets. You type in the numbers you want to explore, and nothing leaves the app.
How does Kid Mode handle children's data?add
The same way as the rest of the app: it collects nothing. Kid Mode is a local, offline savings world with no profiles, no sign-in, and no data collection.
