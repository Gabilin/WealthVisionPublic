4 / 4 — Key Takeaway
Peace of mind
has a price
And it's worth every penny.
1
Start with $1,000 — a starter fund handles most small emergencies. Build to 3-6 months over time.
2
Automate it — set up an auto-transfer on payday. Even $50/week adds up to $2,600/year.
3
Don't invest it — emergency funds shouldn't be in stocks. The point is instant access, not growth.
4
Replenish after use — if you dip in, rebuild it immediately. It's a renewable shield, not a one-time cushion.
