Straight answers

Questions people actually ask

How do you make money with no ads and no subscription?

A one-time unlock. You pay once for the advanced planning tools and keep them forever. The free tier is genuinely useful; Pro is the power-planner upgrade. No ads, no data selling.

Is it really offline?

Yes. Turn on airplane mode and everything still works, because every calculation runs on-device. There are no accounts and no backend — nothing to phone home.

Is this a budgeting app that tracks my spending?

No — and we want to be upfront. WealthVision doesn't link your bank or import transactions. It's a coach and planner: simulators, a payday routine, affordability checks, lessons, and honest benchmarks. It pairs well next to a tracker, but it's a different job.

Are the projected returns guaranteed?

Never. Projections use historical averages shown with a risk band, and it's an educational tool — not financial advice. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results.

What about my child's data in Kid Mode?

Nothing about your child leaves the phone. No ads, no external links, no purchases inside the kid experience. It's designed so you can hand over your phone without worrying.
