Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

WealthVision was built on a simple promise: your money is nobody's business but yours. This policy explains, in plain language, what that means. The short version is that we do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your data. There is no server to send it to.

Information We Collect

None. WealthVision does not require an account and does not ask for personally identifying information. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts.

The financial figures you enter (goals, contributions, income splits, and simulation inputs) are saved only in local storage on your device so the app can remember your progress between sessions. This information never leaves your device and is never visible to us or anyone else.

How Your Data Is Stored

  • All app data lives locally on your device using standard on-device storage.
  • WealthVision has no servers, no database, and no cloud sync. There is nowhere for your data to be sent.
  • You can erase everything instantly by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it. There is no remote copy to delete.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not use analytics or telemetry services.
  • We do not embed advertising networks or third-party trackers.
  • We do not connect to your bank, hold funds, or process transactions of any kind.
  • We do not build profiles, run experiments on you, or share data with partners.

Network Access

WealthVision is designed to run fully offline. Currency symbols and number formats are read from your device's locale settings with no network request. The app makes no calls to fetch prices, rates, or any external data.

Children's Privacy (Kid Mode)

Kid Mode is a local, offline savings experience for children. Like the rest of WealthVision, it collects no data whatsoever. There are no child profiles, no sign-in, and no information is gathered, stored remotely, or transmitted. WealthVision is not directed at gathering personal information from children, and by design it cannot, because it collects nothing from anyone.

Purchases

WealthVision offers a single, optional, one-time purchase to unlock advanced features. Payments are handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see or store your payment details. Your purchase is validated by the store, and their respective privacy policies govern that transaction.

Educational Disclaimer

WealthVision is an educational and planning tool, not financial advice. All projections are illustrative and based on historical averages. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. Because the app collects nothing, we do not anticipate material changes to how your data is handled.
