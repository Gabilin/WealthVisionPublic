How much money
is "enough"?
Everyone asks this. The 1998 Trinity Study gave us an answer backed by 125 years of market data.
The rule:
4%
Withdraw 4% per year and your money lasts 30+ years
If your investments earn ~7% and you withdraw 4%, the remaining 3% covers inflation. Your nest egg stays intact.
This means you need 25x your annual expenses to retire. That's your Financial Independence number.
